International Indian Public School

Welcome to the cyberspace of International Indian Public School, Riyadh. We at IIPS have achieved a confluence of the advent grade and the archetypal, the modern and the traditional, the local and the global. We venerate tradition yet firmly believe that in order to equip students for more productive lives in the changing world, we need to devise a progressive curriculum. As a result, our students are schooled to fit in effortlessly into any scenario. At IIPS, they are being molded into, to use an oft quoted pharse, global citizens.

Your ward becomes our sacred responsibility once they enter IIPS. We believe in the intrinsic uniqueness of each child as far as learning abilities and talents are concerned. We are committed to the happy task of ferreting, stimulating and developing the talent and capabilities of our students. Our faculty members are not mere purveyor of facts but are rather mentors and facilitators who use the latest techniques of pedagogy to motivate students to explore, acquire and assimilate knowledge.
